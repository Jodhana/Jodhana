About Us

JODHANA ~ The Taste Of Marwar is established since 1999 with an office in Jodhpur (Rajasthan, India). It is an eminent firm engaged in Manufacturing and Exporting a good range of Indian Spices. The offered range of products consists of Whole Spices, Ground Spices, Blended Spices, Sharbat & Pickles. Our products are handmade & we have not used any type of harmful chemicals or preservatives. Apart from this, our clients can avail of our products at a reasonable price.

We are expanding rapidly to succeed in intent on our customers pan India. We have enforced international standards in production and maintained a high level of hygiene and quality standards. We have maintained transparency in our business model and policies with our customers.

Our Philosophy

The aim of Jodhana has always been helping every soul feel empowered to get the perfect taste in food and win appreciation for the same. In other words, it's a friend of every soul to give that ultimate satisfaction of the family loving and enjoying the food.

Vision & Mission

Jodhana Spices believes in long-lasting relationships with our clients, our vendors, and our family of workers. Our vision is to supply premium unadulterated products with the very best degree of hygiene in every household.

Food Safety:- Where often our competitors will compromise quality for the price, we will never compromise when it comes to quality. All of our products are inspected using a robust quality control process, where we make sure our quality doesn’t just meet your expectations but exceeds them.

Quality Assurance

Ensuring the integrity of our food safety is our highest priority. We at Jodhana Spices seek to reduce the risks throughout the process and work with exact precision.

Quality Suppliers

Our relationships with the best growers around the world assure the purity and safety of our spices. We employ the strictest criteria in selecting qualified suppliers to supply the very best quality spices.

ACHAAR :
The Original Love is eating home’s pickle...

Achar, also known as Indian pickles, have been a part of the culture and history for 4,000 years. I would argue that the technique of pickling originated in India. The process is as ancient as our civilization. People first started salting and curing food in brines to preserve it for long journeys. Pickling is an ancient food preservation technique. People can make pickles from almost any food. Our pickles do have an array of health benefits. Pickles have a high nutritional value as a rich source of fiber, and the better part is that picking won't impact the nutritional content of the fiber.
